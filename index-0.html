MSP Model
We provide Regular Maintenance, Backup and Disaster Recovery, Email Security, Endpoint Security and much more in a Managed Service Provider model. The MSP model help companies to manage the budget for their IT spending. Also, customers can focus on the services that matters the most and choose the subscriptions on a monthly, quarterly or yearly basis. We provide our customers with a complete insight of their IT by providing weekly/monthly reports along with recommendation on improvements based on their existing infrastructure performance.

Maintenance Contract
Regular maintenance is essential for both hardware and software as the vendor releases patches, updates and firmware on a regular basis to keep their product stable and secure. Our online monitoring platform ensures that updates are installed on time and the system is in production. The customer need not worry about identifying the faults or monitoring the health of the hardware or the environmental situations of the hardware as our software platform takes care of all these tasks.
