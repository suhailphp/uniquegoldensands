IT Security starts with Email Security

  • January 21, 2020
  • admin
  • Strategy

Despite the growth in more targeted attacks through other vectors, email is still the most common channel for opportunistic and targeted attacks, as well as a significant source of data loss
Gartner

As cyber threats continue to take new shapes, the most widely used medium is Email. Email messages pretending to be from known sources are attached with URLs that when clicked will download malicious programs or scripts from the attacker’s server. The attacker then takes control over the victim’s PC and steals information like saved passwords and email addresses from applications and web browsers among other things.

A focus on protecting this kind of email threat one requires a cyber security system that can detect and block URL-based emails. A system that requires advanced threat intelligence that outsmarts attackers.

The first step of IT Security starts with user awareness training. Cybercriminals always use new and improved ways to reach IT resources. Every user needs to be made aware of the important points to take care of before they click on a link in an email even when it comes from known sources.

In 2020, when the pandemic hit the world and people were struggling to cope with the crisis, cyber criminals rejoiced. They struck millions of internet users across the world through email campaigns and different channels pretending to be health workers or bank authorities. Due to the pandemic, many organizations were forced to switch to mobile platforms or work from home options without any preparations. This opened a wide variety of options for the cyber criminals to operate successfully. Among the various options they chose, email remained the biggest choice.
